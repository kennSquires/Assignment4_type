A Cat's Language

Intent & Process

The intention for my final class assignment of Advanced Web; Typography & Animation is to portray cats' personalities through imagery, typography, color and animations. Cats are certainly passive aggressive animals with strong boundaries and many needs, which I tried to show through the playful typography and light color choices. I decided to only do line drawings of cats to allow for the type/color to stand out and rather let the imagery support the animations. My process while designing the website was to first draw a wireframe to confirm my ideas, then I moved forward with my final drawings. After they were complete, I focused on finding variable fonts that would express my concept. The final step that I took to complete the project was to code the layout and animations for my website.



All imagery was drawn by Kennedy Mitchell.
